How it works

Each note in the guitar tab is converted to its pitch (MIDI value), then mapped to the closest fret position on the mandolin.

  • Notes below the mandolin's range (below G3) are automatically transposed up one octave.
  • Notes that can't be reached even after transposition are dropped with a warning.
  • The converter picks the lowest fret available for each note.
  • Maximum fret used: 15.
